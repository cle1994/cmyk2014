Innovative Design, Cal's premier graphic design, photography, videography, and web design club, is hosting a design competition for all student designers. Form a group of four, design for local Bay Area businesses, and receive formal mentorship from professional designers around the Bay! Admission includes food and swag. Judges and mentors from Pixar, Turner Duckworth, Zipfly, and Adobe. Stay tuned for more information!
Should I attend?
Yes! We welcome designers of all skill levels! CMYK will be hosting workshops led by designers during the event. We've allocated time to mingle and form groups with designers of varying skill levels. All you need are ideas, pen and paper, and maybe some illustrator skills! Come learn and sharpen your creativity.
What do we do?
Come meet student designers from UC Berkeley and the Bay Area, learn from some of the best in the field of design, and design under the guidance of peers and professionals! We'll be providing exciting design prompts to get you going, and an expert panel of judges and mentors to help you along the way.
